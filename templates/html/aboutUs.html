Gym Buddy

About Our Application

Welcome to Gym Buddy! If for any reason you are looking for a work out partner, you have found yourself in the right place. Simply fill out a simple form to create an account and you are ready to go. While there might be other applications out there that find you a gym buddy, our application is very unique due to the algorithm we have implemented to find you the best buddy possible using Levenshtein Distances, as computed by our implementation of the Fast Optimal Global Sequence Alignment Algorithm.

About Us

We are a group of students at University of Maryland - Global Campus. For our final capstone class CMSC495, we have decided to implement the Gym Buddy web application. It was a great learning experience and we hope that you enjoy the end product.
