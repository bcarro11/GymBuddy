Gym Buddy: User's Guide

Preface

The "How-To Instructions" below should answer any questions about how to use the application.

FAQs will detail answers to questions that are frequently asked.

We look forward to finding you your new gym buddy.

How-To Instructions

Signing Up

1) The first time you use the application, you will be asked to sign-up. Click the “Create Account” button.

2) Here are all of the fields to be filled in:

- Preferred name (required)

- DOB (required)

- Gender (required)

- Email address (required)

- Password (required)

- Preferred Gym (required)

The password will be inputted twice for verification purposes.

3) A Congratulations page should load to verify your account has been created. In the future, when you log-in using your email address and password, all of your personal information will already be stored for you.

Upload Profile Picture

1) If you want to upload a profile picture, click on the “Upload Picture” button located underneath your current profile picture.

2) You will be taken to a page where you can upload a profile picture from your own computer.

3) A confirmation page will load which verifies your new Profile Picture has succssfully uploaded.

4) Your new profile picture will be displayed on your profile page.

Update Additional Profile Information

1) Besides the required profile information, you can edit the additional profile information section by selecting the “Edit” pencil button at the upper righthand corner of your profile.

2) Enter your answers into the empty fields and then hit the “Save” button. Your new profile will be saved and will now display on your profile page.

Searching For Matches

1) After filling out your profile, to better match you with a buddy with a similar exercise routine, click on the “Search For Buddy” button.

2) You will be allowed to search for a gym buddy by entering the breakdown of the different exercises you will do at the gym by selecting from several dropdown menus what your exercises will be and in what order.

3) Then, click the “Accept” button.

4) All of your matches are users that have selected the same “Preferred Gym” as you. These matches will be listed based upon the person whose workout routine best matches yours using a Levenshtein distance. You can read through the profiles of your matches and message the person who you think is the best fit.

Messaging Your Buddy, Message History and Matching

1) If you click on the “Messages” tab, you can see a log of all of your previous messages sorted by date, with the newest messages at the top of the page.

2) Click on any one of these messages to respond to that potential buddy.

3) After you click on a specific message, a chat window should open for you to message with that buddy where you can get to know them a little bit more and discuss the specific details of a workout session.

4) After you have messaged someone and have agreed to work out together, click the Match! button.

5) A notification will be sent to the person you have matched with and, if they choose to match with you, then both of you will be pulled out of the current user matching pool.

6) Enjoy your workout with your buddy.

While At The Gym

1) It is the default setting that if you are already at the gym but you did not find a match, you will still be in the search pool of potential buddies at your gym.

2) If someone messages you while you are at the gym, you will still receive a notification. You can decide if you want to reply or not to this message depending on how busy you are.

3) If you want to be removed from the search pool, logout of your account or click the “Leave Pool” button on your matches page.

Rating Other Users

1) The application allows you to rate other users on the application.

2) Go to the profile of the user you want to rate and right under their user icon are 5 stars. Click on the star out of 5 you would like to rate that specific buddy and your rating will be submitted and averaged into all of the other ratings.

3) Future users can use this information at their discretion to help determine who they message.

Deleting An Account

1) When you are logged in, click on the “Delete Account” button located in your profile. Once you click that, you will be asked to confirm that you want to move forward with deleting your account.

2) After hitting the confirmation, your account will be taken out of the system.

FAQs

Question: Do you have to have a log-in to use this application?

Answer: Yes, you are required to have a log-in or you cannot use this application.

Question: Is it okay to cancel a gym appointment with a buddy?

Answer: It is possible that you might have a conflict pop up after already confirming with a buddy. Let him/her know you have to cancel by messaging them.

Question: If someone cancels a workout, can you still find another match?

Answer: Yes, just do another search and you will be matched with other buddies that are currently available.

Question: What should I do if I have an issue that is not covered in this user’s guide?

Answer: At the bottom of your Gym Buddy profile page is a link labeled “About Us.” Click that link and you will be taken to a page where you can let us know your issue by filling out a quick form with your name, email address, and question. We will respond to you as quickly as possible.

Question: If I get a bad rating that I think is unfair, can I contest it?

Answer: Yes, you can. Like it states above, you can tell us your problem with your rating by contacting us through the “About Us” form.
