Gym Buddy: User's Guide

Preface

The "How-To Instructions" below should answer any questions about how to use the application.

FAQs will detail answers to questions that are frequently asked.

We look forward to finding you your new gym buddy.

How-To Instructions

Signing Up

1) The first time you use the application, you will be asked to sign-up. Click the “Create Account” button.

2) Here are all of the fields to be filled in:

- First name (required)

- Last name (required)

- Nick name (required)

- DOB (required)

- Gender (required)

- Email address (required)

- Password (required)

- Preferred Gym (required)

The password will be inputted twice for verification purposes.

3) A Congratulations page should load to verify your account has been created. In the future, when you log-in using your e-mail address and password, all of your personal information will already be stored for you.

Upload Profile Picture

1) If you want to upload a profile picture, click on the “Upload Profile Picture” button located underneath your current profile picture.

2) You will be taken to a page where you can upload a profile picture from your own computer.

3) Your new profile picture will be displayed on your profile page.

Update Additional Profile Information

1) Besides the required profile information, you can edit the additional profile information section by selecting the “Edit” pencil button at the upper righthand corner of your profile.

2) Enter your answers into the empty fields and then hit the “Save” button. Your new profile will be saved and will now display on your profile page.

Searching For Matches

1) After filling out your profile, to better match you with a buddy with a similar exercise routine, you will be allowed to search for a gym buddy by entering the breakdown of the different exercises you will do at the gym by ticking all of the exercises you will perform from a list we provide.

2) Then, click the “Search For A Buddy” button.

3) All of your matches are users that have selected the same “Preferred Gym” as you. These matches will be listed based upon the person whose workout routine best matches yours using a Levenshtein distance. You can read through the profiles of your matches and message the person who you think is the best fit.

Messaging Your Buddy And Message History

1) If you click on the “Messages” tab, you can see a log of all of your previous messages sorted by date, with the newest messages at the top of the page.

2) All new, unread messages will be at the top and the unread ones will be bolded. Click on any one of these messages to respond to that potential buddy.

3) After you click on a specific message, a chat window should open for you to message with that buddy where you can get to know them a little bit more and discuss the specific details of a workout session.

4) After you have messaged someone and have agreed on a certain time, one of you will input the time to confirm the workout session. Then, click the “Accept” button. Both of you will receive a message which confirms the time of your workout.

Cancel A Scheduled Workout

1) If you need to cancel your workout session after already confirming a time with your buddy, go to the message between you and them and click “Cancel Workout.”

2) Your buddy will be notified that the workout is canceled. There is a text box where you can enter a reason for canceling if you so wish which your buddy will see.

While At The Gym

1) It is the default setting that if you are already at the gym but you did not find a match, you will still be in the search pool of potential buddies at your gym.

2) If someone messages you while you are at the gym, you will still receive a notification. You can decide if you want to reply or not to this message depending on how busy you are.

3) If you want to be removed from the search pool, logout of your account.

Rating Other Users

1) The application allows you to rate any buddy you have been matched with.

2) Go to the profile of the user you want to rate and right under their user icon are 5 stars. Click on the star out of 5 you would like to rate that specific buddy and your rating will be submitted and averaged into all of the other ratings.

3) Future users can use this information at their discretion to help determine who they message.

Canceling An Account

1) When you are logged in, click on the “Cancel Account” button located in your profile. Once you click that, you will be asked to confirm that you want to move forward with canceling your account.

2) After hitting the confirmation, your account will be taken out of the system.

FAQs

Question: Do you have to have a log-in to use this application?

Answer: Yes, you are required to have a log-in or you cannot use this application.

Question: If I have trouble logging in, what should I do?

Answer: First, you should make sure you are using the correct email address and password to login. If you cannot detect an error and still cannot login, click “Forgot Password?” and you will be asked to enter a new password and verify the new password. Your new password will be saved and you should be able to log-in with your new password.

Question: Is it okay to cancel a gym appointment with a buddy?

Answer: Obviously, things happen in life so this application does have the capability to cancel a scheduled work out as well as let that buddy know the reason for the cancellation.

Question: If someone cancels a workout, can you still find another match?

Answer: Yes, just do another search and you will be matched with other buddies that are currently available.

Question: What should I do if I have an issue that is not covered in this user’s guide?

Answer: At the bottom of the Gym Buddy website is a button labeled “Contact Gym Buddy.” Click that button and let us know your issue. We will respond to you as quickly as possible.
